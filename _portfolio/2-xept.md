[Xept](https://xept.online) is an AI-native academic-writing platform I founded and lead, pairing LLM agents with real-time collaborative LaTeX editing. It has grown to **2,000+ users and 7B+ tokens/month** on a paid-subscription model.

- **Product & multi-agent workflows** — designed the full product, defining multi-agent workflows across the paper lifecycle (literature search, outlining, drafting, data analysis, revision) with cost-aware model routing across Claude, GPT, and Gemini.
- **End-to-end architecture** — built and deployed full-stack infrastructure on GCP (Cloud Run, Cloud SQL/PostgreSQL 16, Artifact Registry, Stripe); scaled from Fly.io to handle 10× concurrent compilation jobs.
- **AI-agent safety layer** — prompt-injection defenses, prompt auditing, and least-privilege access controls, hardened in response to a real prompt-injection incident in production.
