BLADE replaces similarity-score heuristics with multi-source, evidence-based verification powered by LLM-guided analysis, achieving 97.60% precision and 93.74% recall (F1 95.63%) and outperforming the best baseline by +41.83 F1 points. BLADE has been integrated into a commercial SCA product for compliance checks in regulated industries such as automotive.
